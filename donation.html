Your Support Changes Lives

Christian Public Model School is dedicated to providing free education, guidance, and hope to children from underprivileged families. Many students in our community cannot afford school fees, books, uniforms, or transportation. Through the support of generous donors and caring communities, we continue helping children receive quality education in a safe and encouraging environment.

Every contribution helps us improve classrooms, provide learning materials, support teachers, and create better opportunities for students. Our mission is not only to educate children academically, but also to build confidence, discipline, character, and leadership for a brighter future.

We believe education is the strongest way to fight poverty and give hope to families. Your support helps children continue their studies and move toward successful and meaningful lives.
